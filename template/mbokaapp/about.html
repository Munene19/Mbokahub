Mission

We at mbokahub are focused at providing employment opportunities to blue-collar professionals in Kenya and widen their scope into the digital world. From labourers to licenced technicians, mbokahub strives to create a safe platform where almost all forms of blue-collar jobs can be found. We at the same time also promise potential employers access to skilled personell for blue-collar proffessions.
